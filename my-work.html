PowderScout

PowderScout is a storm-chasing trip engine that helps skiers and snowboarders find the best powder for their budget. Enter your dates, home location, and ski passes, and PowderScout ranks destinations based on fresh snow forecasts, travel costs, and lift ticket prices.

powderscout.com ↗

How It Works

  • 1. Enter your trip details: Dates, origin location, skiing mode, and ski passes
  • 2. Ranking algorithm: Evaluates resorts based on fresh snow (hourly forecast data with a custom snow utility curve), affordability (travel costs plus lift ticket prices), and feasibility (drive vs fly decision based on distance)
  • 3. Results: Ranked table of destinations with snowfall, costs, and travel times

Key Features

  • Forecast-driven recommendations: Uses real-time weather forecasts to predict snowfall
  • Multi-pass support: Accounts for Ikon, Epic, and custom ski passes in cost calculations
  • Smart travel modeling: Automatically determines drive vs flight trips and estimates costs
  • Shareable results: Generate links to share recommendations with others
  • Powder alerts (Paid): Create customized alerts for your ideal snow conditions and receive daily email notifications when matching resorts are found
  • Custom passes (Paid): Define your own ski pass configurations with specific resorts, usable days, and blackout dates
  • Community roadmap: Vote on features and suggest improvements (Beta or Paid users)

Pricing

  • Basic Free - Unlimited searches, save preferences, expand top 3 results
  • Pro $25/year - Unlimited result expansions, powder alerts, custom passes, roadmap voting

Tech Stack

  • Frontend Next.js 14 (App Router), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend Next.js API routes, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM
  • Auth Clerk
  • APIs Weather forecasts (Meteoblue/Tomorrow.io), Flight pricing (Amadeus), Google Places/Routes
